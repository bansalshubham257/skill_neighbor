Privacy Policy for SkillNeighbor

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SkillNeighbor ("we," "our," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our neighborhood skill-sharing application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use SkillNeighbor, we collect:

  • Account Information: Username and email address provided during registration.
  • Profile & Skill Information: Skills you choose to share, categories, descriptions, rates, and contact information you voluntarily provide.
  • Location Information: Your general neighborhood/society location, used to connect you with nearby neighbors.

2. Important Disclaimer: No User Verification

SKILLNEIGHBOR DOES NOT VERIFY THE IDENTITY, QUALIFICATIONS, OR INTENTIONS OF ANY USER. We provide a platform for neighbors to connect, but we do not perform background checks, verification, or validation of any user. Your interactions with other users, including booking services or sharing personal information, are entirely at your own risk. Please use your best judgment and prioritize your personal safety when interacting with individuals you meet through this platform.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to facilitate neighborhood connections, display nearby skills, manage community requests, and improve our services.

4. Sharing of Information

Your skill information is shared with other users within your neighborhood/society to enable community connections. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide, but no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You may update or delete your profile information, skills, or account at any time through the app settings.

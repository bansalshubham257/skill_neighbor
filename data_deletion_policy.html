SkillNeighbor Data Deletion Policy

At SkillNeighbor, we are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you full control over your personal information.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your SkillNeighbor account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@skillneighbor.app (please replace this with your actual support email).
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, please provide your registered username for verification purposes.

Once we verify your identity, We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon successful deletion of your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our active databases:

  • User Profile: Username, email address, phone number, and location data.
  • Skills: All skills you have created and listed.
  • Requests: All skill requests sent by you and requests received by you.
  • Society Data: Your membership history and society associations.
  • Tokens: All reward and ad token history.

What Data May Be Retained

For legal, regulatory, and security compliance (e.g., preventing fraud or complying with law enforcement), we may retain minimal non-identifiable transaction logs and security audit logs for a period of up to 90 days before they are permanently purged.

Partial Data Deletion

If you do not wish to delete your entire account, you can manage your data directly within the app. You may delete individual skills or specific request entries at any time through the "My Skills" or "Requests" sections in your profile.

Questions?
If you have any questions regarding your data or this policy, please reach out to us at support@skillneighbor.app.
